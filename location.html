About The Venue

The Palace Garden Hotel Beijing is located approximately 38 km (24 mi) from Beijing Capital International Airport.Diners enjoy Chinese food at the hotel restaurant. In their spare time, guests can unwind with a drink in the bar. Recreational facilities include a gym and an indoor swimming pool. Those wanting to relax can make use of the hot spring. Palace Garden Hotel Beijing provides Wi-Fi in public areas as well as parking on site.

Palace Garden Hotel Beijing offers numerous on-site facilities to satisfy even the most discerning guest. Never be out of touch with your contacts, with free Wi-Fi offered throughout your stay. If you need a ride from or to the airport, the resort can arrange it before your check-in date. Taxi, car hire and shuttle services provided by the resort make it easy to plan your day trips, sightseeing, and more around Beijing.
